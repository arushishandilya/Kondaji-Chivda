About Us

Established in the year 1918, by Late. Kondaji Gunaji Wavare, in Nashik(Gangaghat)

Kondaji Chivada Nashik Special offers a delectable blend of crunchy chivda infused with the bold flavors of onion and garlic. This special chivda, renowned for its unique taste, combines crispy rice flakes with aromatic spices to deliver a savory, irresistible snack. Perfect for enjoying with tea or as a flavorful addition to any meal.oIt's made of puffed rice, edible oil and a choice of spices which cannot be imitated and has been a recipe for more than a century.

A widely popular mix, famous, since 1918, for its tongue teasing taste & Authentic flavour. No artificial colour,No Flavour,No preservative. Roasted poha,Refined edible vegetable oil,Groundnuts,Roasted Gram,Sugar,Spices, Coconut, Cocum, fried onion, garlic ,Salt.

Our Story

Kondaji Chivda is not just a brand; it’s a legacy that began in the heart of Nashik. Since 1920, we have been crafting the finest chivda, blending traditional recipes with modern processes to bring you unparalleled taste and quality.
