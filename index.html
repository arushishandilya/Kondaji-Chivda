Since Year 1918

We are Kondaji Chivda

Established in the year 1918, by Late. Kondaji Gunaji Wavare, in Nashik(Gangaghat)

Kondaji Chivada Nashik Special offers a delectable blend of crunchy chivda infused with the bold flavors of onion and garlic. This special chivda, renowned for its unique taste, combines crispy rice flakes with aromatic spices to deliver a savory, irresistible snack.

Our Story

Kondaji Chivda is not just a brand; it’s a legacy that began in the heart of Nashik. Since 1920, we have been crafting the finest chivda, blending traditional recipes with modern processes to bring you unparalleled taste and quality.
